Skye is an enthusiastic,positive girl, who started drawing at the age of 3-4. With ups and downs she has found what important is in life. At the age of 18 she is graduated at the Academy of Aalst. Now that her life has only just begun she has chosen to specialize her even more in painting and started a course at the Luca School of Arts in Ghent. Painting has taught her to look in a different way, in wich color plays a leading role and brings her to new insights.

She makes most of the time portraits of people around her, thereby trying to express emotions or state of mind based on use of color. Although she starts with the inner state of mind of the model, it is remarkable that her own state of mind also becomes reality in her paintings. Al these aspects make her work personal and expressive. Each painting is a beautiful representation of how she looks at the place, the person and the moment.
